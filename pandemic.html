 PANDEMIC







IN AN ATTEMPT TO  COMPLETELY MIRE US
THE DEVIL SENT THE  COVID 19    VIRUS
BUT GOVERNMENTS TOOK SOME MEASURES
TO CLOSE DOWN ALL OF OUR PLEASURES
THE CHURCHES COULDN’T CONGREGATE
UNTIL THEY THOROUGHLY INVESTIGATE
HOW TO OVERCOME THAT NEUTER
BY CONDUCTING SERVICES ON THE COMPUTER
BUT THE POWERS THAT BE FORGOT TO MUZZLE
THE OLD FASHIONED CROSSWORD PUZZLE
SO ALL IS NOT COMPLETELY FUTILE
YOU STILL CAN USE YOUR BRAIN TO NOODLE
